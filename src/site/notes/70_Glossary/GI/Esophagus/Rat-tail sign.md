- **Narrow, abrupt** distal tapering
- Often **shorter segment**
- May have **irregularity/shouldering** or mucosal abnormality
- Used more for **[[70_Glossary/GI/Esophagus/Pseudoachalasia\|Pseudoachalasia]]** than pure idiopathic achalasia.

Not to be confused with **[[70_Glossary/GI/Esophagus/Bird beak sign\|Bird beak sign]]** which is associated with true [[achalasia\|achalasia]].
