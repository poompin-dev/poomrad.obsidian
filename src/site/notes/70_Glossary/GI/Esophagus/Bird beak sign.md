Exam word for **true** [[achalasia\|achalasia]]

- Smooth, symmetric **tapering** of distal oesophagus at the GOJ
- Long segment, no mucosal destruction
- Dilated food-filled oesophagus proximally
- Classic for **primary achalasia**

Not to be confused with **[[70_Glossary/GI/Esophagus/Rat-tail sign\|Rat-tail sign]]** which is associated with [[70_Glossary/GI/Esophagus/Pseudoachalasia\|Pseudoachalasia]]/malignant stricture.
