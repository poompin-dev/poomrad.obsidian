![Double stripe sign.jpg|400](/img/user/99_assets_Private/Double%20stripe%20sign.jpg)
**Diffuse, symmetrical increased uptake** along metadiaphysial cortex of long bones, usually bilateral tibias.

Seen in both [[30_Rad Knowledge/MSK/Bone/Bony lesions/Sclerotic lesions/Primary hypertrophic osteoarthropathy\|Pachydermoperiostosis]] and [[30_Rad Knowledge/MSK/Bone/Bony lesions/Sclerotic lesions/Secondary hypertrophic osteoarthropathy\|Secondary hypertrophic osteoarthropathy]].

also called "tramline sign" or "parallel track sign"
