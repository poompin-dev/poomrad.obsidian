## Basics
- **Cranial caudal (CC)**
	- Nipple must be in profile and pointing straight
- **Medial lateral oblique (MLO)**
	- Nipple must be in profile
	- The lower edge of the pectoralis muscle must be at or below PNL
	- Inframammary fold should be visualized.
	- Relaxed (convex) pectoralis is preferred (showing more breast tissue).

>**Posterior Nipple Line (PNL)**
>PNL on CC must be within 1 cm of the same measurement of the MLO view.
