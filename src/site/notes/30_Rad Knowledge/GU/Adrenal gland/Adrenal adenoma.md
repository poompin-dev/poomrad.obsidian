## Imaging pearls
- 80% of adrenal adenomas are “lipid-rich,” meaning the presence of lipid can help distinguish adrenal adenomas from other adrenal masses, such as metastases or pheochromocytomas.
- Low intensity in both T1 and T2WI (metas have high T2)
