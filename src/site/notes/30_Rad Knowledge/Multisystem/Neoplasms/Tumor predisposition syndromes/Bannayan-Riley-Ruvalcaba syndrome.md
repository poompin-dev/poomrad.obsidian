## Core features
  - Macrocephaly
  - Developmental delay / hypotonia
  - Multiple subcutaneous lipomas, haemangiomas
  - Intestinal hamartomatous polyps
## Musculoskeletal manifestations
  - Hypotonia
  - Scoliosis
  - Pectus excavatum
  - Joint hyperextensibility
## Other classic clue
  - Pigmented macules of the glans penis (in males)
