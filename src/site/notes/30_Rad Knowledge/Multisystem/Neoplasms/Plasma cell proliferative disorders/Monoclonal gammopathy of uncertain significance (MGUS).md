Part of [[30_Rad Knowledge/Multisystem/Neoplasms/Plasma cell proliferative disorders/Plasma cell proliferative disorders\|plasma cell proliferative disorders]]

Precursor to multiple myeloma

- Abnormal M-protein detected (serum or urine)
- Bone marrow: **<10%** clonal plasma cells
- **No CRAB** features (no hypercalcemia, renal dysfunction, anemia, or bone lesions)
- Risk of progression to MM: **~1% per year**
