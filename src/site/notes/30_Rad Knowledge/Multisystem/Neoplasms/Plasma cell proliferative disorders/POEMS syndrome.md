Plasma cell dyscrasia with
- Polyneuropathy (sensorimotor)
- Organomegaly
	- Hepatosplenomegaly
	- Lymphadenopathy
- Endocrinopathy
	- Diabetes meliitus
	- Amenorrhea
	- Gynecomastia
	- Impotence
	- Hypothyroidism
- M protein
- Skin changes
	- Skin thickening
	- Hyperpigmentation
	- Papilledema
	- Episodes of anasarca

Imaging:
 - peculiar pattern of bone proliferation consists of fluffy, spiculated, hyperostotic areas.
 - predilection for the vertebral column and pelvis.
