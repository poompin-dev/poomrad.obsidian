[[30_Rad Knowledge/Neuro/Head and neck/Paragangliomas of the head and neck\|Paragangliomas of the head and neck]] at middle ear cavity, seen as small enhancing mass on **cochlear promontory**; jugular foramen intact.
## Differential diagnosis
- **Aberrant ICA/ high-riding jugular bulb:** follow vascular anatomy and enhance like vessels.
- **Cholesteatoma:** non-enhancing (or restricted diffusion), erosive but not hypervascular.
