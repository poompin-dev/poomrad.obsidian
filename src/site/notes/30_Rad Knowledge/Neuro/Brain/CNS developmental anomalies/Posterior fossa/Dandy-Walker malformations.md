## Pathogenesis
- Congenital anomaly thought to arise from abnormal development of the rhombencephalon
- Result in abnormal expansion of the forth ventricle
- Prevent normal caudal migration of the tentorium and torcular herophili

## Key features
- Marked vermian hypoplasia/agenesis
- Cystic dilatation of the fourth ventricle
- Torcular-lambdoid inversion
  (Torcular lies too far superiorly, cranial to the lambdoid suture)
